Flight Ticket Reservation

User can search through our list of available flights and find the one that suits him.
He chooses one or more seat on the plane for that flight and invites friends if he chooses to.
User will have an option to make hotel reservation or rent-a-car reservation at the place he is visiting.

Hotel Reservation

Registered user can choose a hotel from a list or search for it using criteria provided.
He can choose specifics like date, number of nights spent, number of guests that are accompanying him and the price span.

Rent-a-Car Reservation

User chooses rent-a-car service or searches for it.
He can choose specifics like date and place of pick up, date and place of giving a car back, type of car, number of passengers.
Optionally, he can define a price span.

Frequently Ask Questions

What is Reservify?

Reservify is a service that is there to accommodate all your travel needs!

You'll be able to make reservations for flight tickets, hotels and rent-a-car services in the places you visit.

Is this available to my country?

Reservify is available to all countries with airports and airlines that are operating there.

How do I use the features of Reservify?

You will be able to search for flight tickets and destinations using advanced filter and search criteria provided. You can invite friends and reserve hotel or rent-a-car service cars at the place you visit.

How much do the Reservify services cost?

Reservify is completely free since a small group of students made it for a sole purpose to hone their programming skills.

I have technical problem, who do I email?

You can reach our customer support at customers@reservify.com. We will get back to you shortly.
